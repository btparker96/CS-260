I was inspired to be a photographer when I realized the beauty of the tabernacle at Temple Square in Salt Lake City, Utah. While I was there, not only did the beauty of the scene leave such an impression on my mind but the entire moment--the light reflecting off the organ pipes, the music from the choir, the close friends nearby, and what would take another thousand words to describe. I wanted to do something that would capture the moment--preserve the experience and enhance the memory. That is when I was inspired to become a photographer--a quality picture truly says a thousand words! Starting Tabernacle Photography has been a joy. The people I have met and for which I have had the opportunity to help create lasting experiences has been so fulfilling. I hope to meet many more to come!
